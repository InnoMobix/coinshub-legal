Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

CoinsHub ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the CoinsHub mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

CoinsHub collects only the minimum data required for the app to function.

  • Authentication data: If you sign in using Apple/Google, we receive basic profile information such as your email address and display name.
  • User-generated content: Coins, photos, and collection data you add to the app.
  • Technical data: Anonymous technical information required by Firebase services (e.g. crash reports).

2. How We Use Information

We use your data solely to:

  • Provide app functionality
  • Sync your collections across devices
  • Improve stability and performance

3. Data Storage

Data is stored securely using Google Firebase services (Firestore and Firebase Storage). Only you can access your personal collection data.

4. Payments

CoinsHub is completely free. We do not process payments, subscriptions, or in-app purchases.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

6. Data Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from within the app. This will permanently remove your user data from our systems.

7. Children’s Privacy

CoinsHub is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
